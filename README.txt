Proteus Moon 
============

Features:
=========

Start:
======

This requires at first two instances of the game to play. 
The main part is to show off the networking aspect of the game.
To do this just click Multiplayer to join a room.
Wait for another player to join or your other instance of the game.

How to play:
============

W - move forward (camera and player, when selected)
A - move left (camera and player, when selected)
S - move backward (player, when selected)
D - move right (camera and player, when selected)

Left click to focus on your player and drag off a unit allows paning of the camera.

When it is your turn you are alloted option to do with that unit.

Objective:
==========

1) Kill the enemy leader
2) Reach an X amount of resources
